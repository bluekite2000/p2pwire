The product

How it works

  • Dung wants to send 100usd from New York to Toan in Hanoi

    Dung goes to fundvn.com and puts in his bank account info and Toan's bank account info

  • Within 24 hours, money is taken from Dung's bank account

    Once the money gets to our company's US account, we will send Dung an email and a sms to confirm the withdrawal

  • Within 24 hours, Toan's bank account receives the money

    The money will come from our VN's bank account. We will send Toan and email and a sms describing the sender, amount sent,etc.

  • Everything is automatic

    This ensures there is no delay. Our software is very advanced and secure, the best in the market.

  • Everything is electronic

    All transactions are posted online, to be accessed anytime anywhere by both sender and receiver

  • We are licensed and insured

    We have money transfer licenses in both US and VN. This means there is no fraud or theft
