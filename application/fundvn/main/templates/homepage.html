The product

How it works

  • Sender describes himself

    Sender specifies his name, address and bank info (account and routing numbers)

  • Sender describes the receiver

    Sender specifies receiver's name, address and bank info.

  • Sender describes amount sent

    Sender specifies how much he wants to send.

  • Money is transferred to our bank account

    Once we receive the money, we will send an email notification to the sender

  • Receiver receives his money

    Money is now transferred from our account to receiver. We will also send an email notification to the sender

  • Account book-keeping

    Both sender and receiver can log on and see his daily, weekly or monthly transfer

  • Best exchange rate

    Sender can specify USD or VND for receiver. We guarantee the best rate in the market

  • Create an account only one time

    Next time you log on, just type an amount and you are done.
